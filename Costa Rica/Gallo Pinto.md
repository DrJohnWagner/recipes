# Gallo Pinto

Costa Rican black beans and rice, seasoned with Salsa Lizano and fresh cilantro.

**Prep Time:** 10 min | **Cook Time:** 20 min | **Total Time:** 30 min | **Servings:** 8–10

## Ingredients

* 2 Tbsp light-tasting oil (vegetable, mild olive, or canola)
* 1 red bell pepper, chopped
* 1 small yellow onion, chopped
* 2 cloves garlic, minced
* 1 tsp ground cumin
* 2 cups cooked black beans, with 3/4 cup reserved cooking liquid
* 1/4 cup Salsa Lizano, plus more to taste
* 3 cups cooked rice, preferably day-old and refrigerated
* 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped

## Steps

1. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat until shimmering. Sauté bell pepper and onion for 6–8 minutes until the pepper softens and onion turns translucent.
2. Add garlic and cumin. Cook for 1 minute until fragrant.
3. Add black beans, reserved cooking liquid, and Salsa Lizano. Simmer for 5 minutes until slightly thickened.
4. Gently stir in rice and cook for 3–5 minutes until heated through and most of the liquid is absorbed.
5. Stir in cilantro. Season with additional Salsa Lizano if desired and serve.

## Notes

* Day-old refrigerated rice separates more easily and gives a better texture.
* Salsa Lizano is the defining flavour — do not substitute.
