# Chicken Curry

A coconut milk-based Instant Pot curry with chicken thighs, tomato and warming spices.

**Prep Time:** 5 min | **Cook Time:** 25 min | **Total Time:** 30 min | **Servings:** 8

## Ingredients

* 2 Tbsp ghee or oil
* 2 tsp cumin seeds
* 2 cups yellow onion, diced
* 1 Tbsp ginger, grated or paste
* 1 Tbsp garlic, minced or paste
* 2 cups tomato puree (not paste)
* 900 g boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into 3–4 cm pieces
* 1/2 cup water
* 2 cups full-fat coconut milk
* 2 tsp garam masala
* 2 Tbsp lime juice
* Fresh cilantro, to garnish

### Spices

* 2 tsp ground cumin
* 1 tsp ground turmeric
* 2 tsp Kashmiri red chilli powder or paprika
* 2 tsp coriander powder
* 2 tsp salt

## Steps

1. Set Instant Pot to sauté. Add ghee and cumin seeds; cook for 30 seconds until sizzling.
2. Add onion, ginger and garlic. Sauté for about 3 minutes until golden.
3. Add tomato puree and all spices except garam masala. Sauté for 2–3 minutes.
4. Add chicken and sauté for 3–4 minutes, stirring frequently.
5. Add water and deglaze the pot thoroughly.
6. Seal the lid, pressure cook on high for 4 minutes, then quick-release.
7. Stir in coconut milk and garam masala. Sauté for 4–5 minutes until the sauce thickens.
8. Add lime juice, garnish with cilantro, and serve over basmati rice or with naan.

## Notes

* Chicken thighs hold up far better under pressure than breast — use them.
* Use tomato puree, not concentrated paste; the latter will scorch and over-intensify.
* Cauliflower, spinach or peas can be stirred in at step 7 for extra vegetables.
