# Sweet and Spicy Pumpkin Soup

A vibrant pureed pumpkin soup with chilli heat and a garnish of roasted pumpkin seeds.

## Ingredients

* 1 kg pumpkin
* 2 large red onions
* 2 carrots
* 3 stalks celery
* 2 large potatoes
* 2 red capsicum
* 2 to 3 chillies
* 1 tsp Hungarian sweet paprika
* 2 Tbsp brown sugar
* 1 cup vegetable broth

## Steps

1. Wash and dice the pumpkin skin-on, setting the seeds aside. Wash, de-seed and dice the capsicum and chillies. Wash and dice all remaining vegetables.
2. Toss everything into the Instant Pot and cook on high pressure for 5 minutes.
3. While the soup cooks, clean the pumpkin seeds and toss with a little olive oil. Lay on parchment paper, sprinkle lightly with salt and Hungarian sweet paprika, and bake at 350°F (175°C) for 10–12 minutes until golden brown. Watch closely — they burn quickly.
4. When the Instant Pot is done, release the pressure manually. Purée with a hand blender.
5. Pour into bowls, sprinkle with Hungarian sweet paprika, and garnish with roasted pumpkin seeds.
