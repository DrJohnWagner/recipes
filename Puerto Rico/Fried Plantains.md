# Fried Plantains

**Prep Time:** 2 min | **Cook Time:** 5 min | **Total Time:** 7 min | **Servings:** 2

## Ingredients

* 2 very ripe plantains, peeled and sliced diagonally into 1/2-inch pieces
* 2 Tbsp coconut oil, or enough to coat the pan
* Kosher salt and black pepper, to taste

## Steps

1. Heat coconut oil in a heavy-bottomed skillet over medium-high heat until very hot.
2. Add plantain slices in a single layer. Sprinkle generously with salt and pepper.
3. Fry for about 2 minutes until golden brown on the underside.
4. Flip, sprinkle the second side with salt and pepper, and fry for another 1–2 minutes until golden brown.
5. Transfer to a paper towel to drain. Serve warm.

## Notes

* Plantains must be very ripe — yellow with heavy black spotting. Under-ripe plantains are starchy and will not caramelise properly.
* Fry in batches if needed; overcrowding drops the oil temperature and stews rather than fries.
* Reheat leftovers in a 350°F (175°C) oven for 5–7 minutes. Avoid the microwave.

## Tips

* A squeeze of lime at the end cuts the sweetness cleanly.
* Ghee or avocado oil work well in place of coconut oil.
